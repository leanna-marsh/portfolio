Having spent my life in Vancouver, Frankfurt, and Los Angeles, I approach my professional and personal life with flexibility and open-mindedness.


This flexibility is apparent through my work experience.

Someone once compared my career path as a bowling ball traveling down the lane; bouncing off of the bumpers toward the pins. Though I have made career pivots along my way, each step has led me down a linear path toward technical design.

Despite a shift in title or industry, my previous experience has provided me a solid foundation to succeed. With roles in Marketing, Film, Technology, and Design, I've gained all of the pieces to make me excel.

When I design, I am able to see the big picture instead of focusing on a small piece of the puzzle and I enjoy working with clients and other departments in order for our designs to come to life within the constraints of our parameters.
