Hi, I'm Leanna.
I bring an extensive background in Marketing and Project Management to my design process. My goal is to create product solutions where client and user's needs collide.

Though I recognize that the design process shifts from project to project, it always starts with a problem and works toward a solution.

I believe that User Experience is more than aesthetic-focused design; it's about recognizing the goals of the company and the goals of the users and bringing those together in a fun and cohesive space.
